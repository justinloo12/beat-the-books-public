- **No official picks yet.** The current strategy (mid-band totals) produced zero board picks

  closing-line capture now in the pipeline and needs multi-snapshot days to mean anything.

  only), not the true market close.
- **Archive completeness:** only days on which the refresh workflow ran and validated are
  present. Days the pipeline failed are absent, which is a form of survivorship in the
  archive itself.
